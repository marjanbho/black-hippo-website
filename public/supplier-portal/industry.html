A platform designed specifically for the realities of seasonal collections, supplier complexity, and margin pressures in home & decoration.

The reality of seasonal collections

Managing seasonal assortments means juggling tight timelines, multiple suppliers, and constantly changing product ranges.

Compressed Timelines

Spring/Summer and Fall/Winter collections leave little room for delays or rework.

  • 6-month development cycles
  • Fixed market entry dates
  • No time for scattered information

Multiple Suppliers

Working with multiple international suppliers across different categories and regions.

  • Different communication styles
  • Varying quality standards
  • Complex logistics coordination

Changing Ranges

Product ranges evolve throughout development as pricing, margins and market feedback change.

  • Supplier price negotiations
  • Landed cost calculation and budget control
  • Margin monitoring and adjustments

Common decision bottlenecks

The challenges that slow down every collection and create unnecessary risk.

Scattered Information

Supplier details in emails, product specs in spreadsheets, samples in different locations, and compliance documents somewhere else.

Time lost searching for information
Decisions made with incomplete data
Incomplete and inconsistent supplier data

Compliance Surprises

Discovering missing certifications or documentation issues late in the process, when it's expensive to fix.

Last-minute compliance issues
Costly product replacements
Delayed market entry

Margin Drift

Costs change throughout the development process without clear visibility into how they impact margins and overall budget.

Hidden cost increases
Margin erosion without visibility
Budget overruns discovered too late
