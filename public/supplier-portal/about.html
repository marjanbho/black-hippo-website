Why Supplier Portal exists

Decision clarity for seasonal assortments shouldn't be this complicated.

The problem we set out to solve

We watched home & decoration wholesalers struggle with the same challenge repeatedly: making confident assortment decisions while information was scattered across emails, spreadsheets, and separate systems.

Every seasonal collection became an exercise in information gathering rather than strategic decision-making. Teams spent more time hunting for data than analyzing it.

We believed there had to be a better way to connect product, supplier, and budget decisions in one coherent flow.

Our principles

How we approach building solutions for complex assortment decisions.

Stable

We build for the long term. Your assortment processes need reliability, not constant change.

  • Consistent performance across seasons
  • Predictable workflows
  • Dependable integrations

Structured

Clear frameworks that guide decision-making without constraining creativity or flexibility.

  • Logical information hierarchy
  • Guided decision workflows
  • Clear accountability paths

Integrated

Works with your existing systems rather than replacing them. Strengthens what you already have.

  • ERP system compatibility
  • Data flow continuity
  • Minimal disruption approach
